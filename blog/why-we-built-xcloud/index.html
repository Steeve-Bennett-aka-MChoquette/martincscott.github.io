Our Vision: WPDeveloper aimed to streamline WordPress Hosting

Here at WPDeveloper, WordPress is in our hearts and at the core of our wide range of products and plugins. Since the very beginning, we have been contributing to this open-source project by developing and maintaining innovative solutions.

But even with all the products to simplify web-building on WordPress, setting up a cloud server, hosting a website, and later managing the deployed sites is not always smooth and straightforward – especially if you are new to coding. And when it came to hosting WordPress websites and managing our servers, we have faced many challenges, just like you. Besides, when you have multiple websites to manage, we feel that a streamlined server management solution is still missing.

That’s when the idea of xCloud popped up in our heads with the aim of simplifying WordPress hosting and server management like never before for seasoned users as well as newcomers. For the last two years, we have worked hard to make this vision come to life, and finally, it is ready to present to you and share the experience of next-level hosting solutions.
The founder of WPDeveloper, M Asif Rahman, believes that with the right hosting and server management tool in your hand, you can not only enhance your website-building experience but also accelerate your online success.

M Asif Rahman

Founder, WPDeveloper & xCloud

I have been managing WordPress websites for nearly 20 years. So, I have observed how the hosting process in WordPress has rapidly changed. The main issue I consistently faced was managing all the websites I had. I constantly asked, “When hosting too many websites— how does one manage all of those from a single place?”

And I also wondered where you could have the freedom to use your own cloud system with full flexibility. So, I wanted to create something that could serve my own needs, allowing me to use and build websites without any hassle smoothly.

This is why we built xCloud, with a vision that it could serve the best purpose for myself and millions of other users in WordPress. It offers the freedom to utilize the best possible technology with peace of mind, eliminating concerns about whether it’s actually being maintained properly.

What Is xCloud: Next-Gen Cloud Hosting & Server Management

xCloud is a cloud hosting and server management solution, designed to simplify the way you host, deploy, and manage WordPress websites.

With this next-gen WordPress cloud hosting platform, you no longer need to be a code expert to configure your server and manage your websites. Say goodbye to manual setup and automate server configuration, WordPress installation, website migration, server monitoring, plugin updates, and more with ease.
