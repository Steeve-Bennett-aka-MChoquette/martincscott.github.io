5 Best Free+Pro Autoresponder Tools For WordPress In 2025

Published on June 10, 2023

An autoresponder is an email marketing tool that automates the process of sending emails to subscribers on a predetermined schedule or trigger. Autoresponder tools can help businesses save time, nurture leads, and increase customer engagement. In today’s digital landscape, email marketing is still one of the most effective ways to reach and engage with customers. It can help businesses build relationships with their audience, drive traffic to their website, and increase sales. In this article, we will introduce the 5 best free and pro autoresponder tools for WordPress in 2025.

5 Best Free+Pro Autoresponder Tools For WordPress

Email marketing is a crucial component of any successful digital marketing strategy, and autoresponder tools are an essential part of that strategy. Below are the 5 best free and pro autoresponder tools for WordPress in 2023 that offer a range of features and pricing options to suit businesses of all sizes.

1. MailChimp

Mailchimp is a powerful autoresponder tool that has been around for many years, making it a popular choice for businesses of all sizes. With its free plan allowing up to 2000 subscribers, Mailchimp is a great option for small businesses looking to get started with email marketing. Its feature set includes automation, segmentation, A/B testing, and more, making it easy to create and send targeted campaigns that engage your audience.

Mailchimp offers an autoresponder plugin that you can integrate easily. This WordPress autoresponder tool allows you to connect your Mailchimp account to your website, as well as integrate with popular form plugins like Gravity Forms, Contact Form 7, and more. This seamless integration makes it easy to collect new subscribers, add them to your mailing list, and automate email campaigns.

Mailchimp autoresponder plugin offers a range of plans to suit businesses of all sizes. The free plan may be suitable for growing your business initially. You may consider upgrading to the Essential, Standard, or Premium plans as needed. These plans offer more advanced features like multivariate testing, retargeting ads, and predictive insights.

Mailchimp offers a comprehensive feature set, which includes everything you need to run successful email campaigns. Additionally, Mailchimp is incredibly user-friendly, making it easy for even the most inexperienced users to create and send professional-looking emails. Finally, Mailchimp is highly scalable, making it a great option for businesses of all sizes.

However, there are some limitations to the free plan when it comes to customization options. So businesses looking for more advanced features may need to upgrade to a paid plan. Nevertheless, Mailchimp remains a top choice for businesses looking for a reliable, easy-to-use WordPress autoresponder tool that offers a set of useful features and seamless integration with WordPress.

2. Sendinblue

If you’re looking for an affordable WordPress autoresponder tool that offers a wide range of features, Sendinblue may be the perfect choice for you. The free plan allows up to 300 emails per day. Sendinblue is a great option for small businesses and individuals who are just starting out with email marketing. The features include SMS marketing, marketing automation, and even chat functionality, making it a versatile tool for engaging with your audience.

For WordPress, Sendinblue offers an autoresponder plugin that makes integration super easy. You can easily connect your Sendinblue account to your WordPress website, as well as integrate with popular form plugins like Gravity Forms, Contact Form 7, and more. 

To match businesses of different sizes, Sendinblue offers a range of plans. The free plan is good to start, but businesses looking for more advanced features can consider upgrading to the Lite, Essential, or Premium plans. These plans offer features like advanced automation, landing pages, and Facebook ads, making them a great option for businesses looking to elevate their email marketing campaigns.

Sendinblue is affordable which makes it a great choice for businesses on a budget. Apart from that, it includes SMS marketing and chat functionality. These are some unique features that set Sendinblue apart from other autoresponder tools. However, businesses using the free plan may find the limited number of email templates to be a drawback, as it can be difficult to create professional-looking emails without a wide range of options.

Sendinblue is worth considering for businesses searching for an affordable, feature-rich WordPress autoresponder plugin. Whether you’re just starting out or looking to take your email marketing to the next level, Sendinblue can be a good option.

3. AWeber

Another popular autoresponder tool is AWeber. For over 20 years, It’s been a trusted choice for businesses of all sizes. The free plan of this popular tool allows for up to 500 subscribers which is another great option for start-up businesses and individuals who are just new to email marketing. List management, email automation, and reporting are some of their mentionable features, making it easy to create and send targeted campaigns that engage your audience.

This autoresponder tool offers an official autoresponder plugin for WordPress that makes integration a breeze. Connecting your AWeber account to your WordPress website is very easy. You can also integrate with popular form plugins. This makes it easy to collect new subscribers, add them to your mailing list, and automate email campaigns.

The pricing involves both free and pro plans. While the free plan is a great starting point, businesses looking for more advanced features can opt for the pro plan. Advanced automation and landing pages are some features among many that make it a good choice for businesses seeking to take their email marketing to the next level.

AWeber has a simple setup process making it easy for even the most inexperienced users to get started with email marketing. On top of that, AWeber offers a wide range of rich email templates, which can help businesses create professional-looking emails that engage their audience. In addition to that, AWeber provides top-notch customer support, offering helpful resources and responsive support for any issues that may arise.

Businesses using the free plan may find the limited automation features, which might be a drawback. It can be difficult to create more complex email campaigns without these advanced features. Despite this fact, AWeber can be a reliable, easy-to-use autoresponder tool for businesses.

4. Get Response (PRO)

GetResponse is an all-in-one marketing platform that offers a wide range of features to help businesses grow and engage with their audience. While there is no free plan available, GetResponse does offer a 30-day free trial, giving businesses the chance to test out the platform and see if it’s the right fit for their needs. Its feature set includes landing pages, webinars, and advanced automation, making it a comprehensive tool for all your marketing needs.

When it comes to WordPress, GetResponse offers an auto-responder plugin that makes integration a breeze. This plugin allows you to easily connect your GetResponse account to your WordPress website, as well as integrate with popular form plugins. Gravity Forms, Contact Form 7, etc are among the popular ones.

In terms of pricing, GetResponse offers four different plans: Basic, Plus, Professional, and Max. While these plans can be more expensive than some other autoresponder tools, they do offer a vast range of advanced features that can help businesses enhance their marketing efforts. With features like advanced automation, webinars, and landing pages, GetResponse is a powerful tool for businesses looking to grow and engage with their audience.

GetResponse is an efficient autoresponder tool that allows businesses to manage all their marketing needs from one place. Its advanced automation features make it easy to create complex email campaigns that engage and nurture your audience. GetResponse’s webinars can be a powerful tool for businesses looking to educate their audience and build authority in their industry.

This autoresponder tool does not offer any free plan. The absence of a free plan may be a drawback for small businesses or those on a tight budget. Additionally, the pricing can be higher than some other autoresponder tools, making it important for businesses to carefully consider their needs and budget before committing to GetResponse. 

5. Moosend

Moosend is a feature-rich autoresponder tool that offers many powerful features at an affordable price. It has both free and pro plans. The free plan allows for up to 1000 subscribers, Moosend is a great option for small businesses and individuals who are just starting out with email marketing. 

For WordPress, Moosend offers an autoresponder plugin that comes with an easy integration process. The features of this powerful autoresponder plugin involve advanced automation, segmentation, and real-time analytics, making it easy to create targeted campaigns that engage your audience.

Its Pro and Enterprise plans offer more advanced features, such as custom branding and priority support, making it a great option for businesses looking to take their email marketing to another level.

Moosend provides a user-friendly interface for users to easily get started with email marketing. Along with that, advanced automation features make it easy to create complex email campaigns that engage and nurture your audience. 

Finally, Moosend’s affordable pricing makes it a great option for businesses looking for a powerful autoresponder tool that won’t break the bank. However, businesses may find the limited template library and integrations to be a drawback, especially if they require a wider range of design options or need to integrate with specific apps. 

It’s important for businesses to carefully consider their email marketing needs and budget when choosing an autoresponder tool. While each of the 5 autoresponder tools mentioned here has its own strengths and weaknesses, they all offer powerful features and integration with WordPress.

Reshape Your Email Marketing Strategy With The Right Autoresponder Tools

Businesses that take advantage of email marketing and utilize the power of autoresponder tools have better chances to engage with their audience. It unleashes the opportunity to drive business growth in this increasingly competitive digital landscape. So, choose the autoresponder tool that best fits your business needs and budget, and unlock the power of email marketing today!
