A Journey to Growth: What Was the Idea Behind BetterDocs?

Back in 2019, we created BetterDocs with a few features that a good knowledge base should have. Within this journey of 5.5 years, with many features and growth opportunities, we have achieved 40,000+ happy users and strive to achieve more. 

Initially, the idea was to help our WordPress users with customer support. A well-maintained documentation management system is important to make the customer support process more manageable and smoother. With this idea, BetterDocs initially set out to create a sticky Table of contents, preexisting streamlined templates, etc. 

Later on, we thought about how we could take this knowledge base to a whole new level by adding different customization options and trendy features. So we listened to our users and tried to understand their needs. 

Keeping these requirements in mind, we introduced features like internal and multiple knowledge bases, document customization options, AI chatbot support & writing feature, FAQ builder, advanced analytics and many more. 

Our main goal is to be the one-stop solution for document management for our WordPress users. So, we are trying and working every day with your support to make this knowledge base plugin better. 

How Did Growth Happen Over the Years?

First, the main goal was to make the support system easy. As a result, we started BetterDocs by creating only documents with customization options. But then we thrive with the users with a lot of amazing features. 

Hitting 10,000+ users was one of our significant achievements. Because in less than one year, we have hit 10,000 happy users, a notable achievement in terms of a documentation plugin. 

We introduced ready document templates with a customization feature, an advanced LIVE search bar, and multiple knowledge base creation. As we keep growing to 20,000+ users, we have improved BetterDocs’ Performance with optimized code and integration with Elementor. 

And in this journey, we introduced sorting documentation with shortcodes, creating an internal knowledge base with ease

While improving these features, we suddenly hit 30,000+ users. In this journey, we have brought sorting documentation with shortcodes, so that you can easily customize your WordPress knowledge base. For better documentation, password-protected documentation is used to maintain better privacy of the knowledge base.  

And in April 2025, we have hit 40,000+ satisfied users while creating more advanced features. With AI features, an AI chatbot addon, analytics and many more, this journey is full of growth. This growth inspires us to make this product look more powerful for your website daily.
