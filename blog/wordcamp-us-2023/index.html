WordCamp US 2023: The WordPress Community Awaits An Exciting Event In Maryland

Published on August 15, 2023

The wait for the biggest WordPress conference in the United States is almost over as WordCamp US 2023 is getting ready to unroll its magic this month, uniting the vibrant WordPress community in the ultimate gathering of minds. WordPress enthusiasts from every corner of the globe are brimming with excitement, all set to participate in this extraordinary WordCamp journey at the National Harbor, Maryland on 24th-26th August 2023. Dive right into our blog below to read about everything you can expect to experience at this amazing event!

WCUS 2023: Get Ready For An Amazing Three-Day Flagship WordPress Event

The stages are all set and the team who are taking charge to make this event a grand success is eagerly waiting to welcome all the enthusiastic WordCampers from all over the world. But before we deep dive into the exciting surprises that WordCamp US 2023 has in store for you, let’s take a quick glimpse at the mesmerizing venue and find out more about the dedicated team behind all of it. 

Organizers And Sponsors Of WordCamp US 2023

The organizers and sponsors of WordCamp US 2023 are leaving no stone unturned to make this event a pleasant success with their exceptional dedication, hard work, and continuous support. Renowned names like Hostinger, Jetpack, Elementor, WooCommerce, Cloudways, and InstaWP are among the proud sponsors of this prestigious event.

The organizing team of WCUS 2023 is working hard to ensure that everyone who attends has a positive experience. From COVID safety concerns to the provision of meals on special dietary concerns, WordCamp US has kept everything in sincere consideration. For anyone who needs a break or for breastfeeding, there will be provision of a Quiet Room and a Nursing Room. The safety and comfort of the attendees will be of utmost importance which is evident.

About The Grand Venue At The Center Of Maryland

Mark your calendars for the 2023 Community Summit and WordCamp US, all ready to take place at the magnificent Gaylord National Resort & Convention Center, nestled in the heart of National Harbor, Maryland. It’s located near the bank of the Potomac River and just a stone’s throw away from the hustle and bustle of Washington, D.C., ensuring seamless connectivity and access. 

Photo credit: Gaylord National Resort & Convention Center Photo Gallery

WordCamp US Uncovered: What’s In Store For You In 2023?

The countdown has already begun for the much-awaited event and WordCamp US 2023 is being set to welcome you all WordPress enthusiasts there. As it’s gearing up for the epic gathering of WordCamp lovers from around the world, let’s take a look at the amazing things that WordCamp US has to offer.

🖥️ When Is The Contributor Day?

Contributor Day is a time for WordPress users to work together on the open-source WordPress project. People like you make and take care of WordPress. Even in just one day, you can make a difference. The Contributor Day of WordCamp US will take place on Thursday, August 24th. 

Everyone is invited to participate in the WCUS 2023 Contributor Day, irrespective of whether you hold a ticket or not. On Contributor Day, you can translate, write documents, fix bugs, test, and do so much more! The Sustainability team which was only launched during the WCEU 2023 will be available at the WCUS event for the first time.

🎙️ Matt Mulengwang & Other Notable Speakers’ Engaging Session

WordCamp US 2023 participants can expect many engaging sessions from notable speakers from around the world. But the true highlight of this event will be the last speaker session of WCUS from the one and only Matt Mullenweg, co-founder of WordPress. 

In the closing presentation on the 2nd day of the WordCamp, he will be discussing the benefits of operating within the open-source platform and explaining why it’s the upcoming backbone of the web.

Get Ready For WordCamp US 2023: Maryland Is Excited To See You

Get to know GeorgiPuu, the official Wapuu for WordCamp US 2023 (WCUS). This mystical creature has been seen riding the bright Ferris wheel late at night on the Potomac River in National Harbour since the early days of the United States, which is across the river from the nation’s capital. 

As we anticipate the vibrancy of WordCamp US 2023, remember that this isn’t just an event—it’s a community gathering, a learning platform, a place of innovation and collaboration. We’re certain of a memorable experience with WordPress lovers from across the world sharing ideas and growing together. So, pack your enthusiasm, load up on your queries and ideas, and get ready to dive into this immersive experience together, combining learning, and networking. Maryland is waiting to greet you!

For the latest updates, please keep your eyes on the official website of WordCamp US 2023 as well as our blog to get more WordCamp news.
